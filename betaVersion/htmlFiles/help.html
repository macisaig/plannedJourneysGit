    Frequently Asked Questions:


    1. Why should I sign up on Voiyag? - By signing up for Voiyag, you gain access to many features. You can save your plans, download your plans for offline use, book and save itineraries, favorite plans created by other members, and more. Plus we will keep you updated on your upcoming trip plans, along with great travel deals!

    2. How do I start planning a trip? - Simply search for a location you want to visit from the home page, or navigate to the Plan a Trip page and start planning it there. Here you can easily add activities and locations as you build your itinerary.

    3. Can I use Voiyag when I am not in phone service? - Yes! Voiyag allows you to download your plans and save it to your phone. While you are within cell service or wifi, simply hit the download button on your plan's page. The full plan, along with every aspect of the itinerary, will be available to you regardless of service.

    4. How is Voiyag different from other travel websites? - Voiyag takes the hassle out of trip planning by helping you create a perfect itinerary in a shorter amount of time. By putting all of the power of search engines and travel websites at your fingertips in a singular location, it's much easier to create a plan that suits your travel desires

    5. Can I purchase my itinerary while on Voiyag? - Yes! We provide the option to purchase the individual parts of your itinerary all in a single location, so that you don't have to run around to each provider.

    6. How can I view plans created by others? - Navigate to the Destinations or Popular Trips pages on the navbar in order to see plans sorted by destinations, or the currently most popular trips. This is a great way to not only get great plans, but also to get great ideas for the future.
