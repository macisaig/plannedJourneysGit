    • Welcome

    We're Voiyag, a creative travel engine that simplifies travel planning

    It started as a Plan. And became a Journey.

    This entire website was created from a passion for travelling, and a desire to cut out the amount of planning necessary for an awesome trip. That drive led to the creation of this travel algorithm, with the goal of making the planning process more streamlined and efficient, while providing results expected from a travel agency.

    Every single time I would plan a new trip, it would take hours of planning to make sure that I had:

    • 1) Found all of the best places and activities to visit in the country
    • 2) Found the best places to stay, usually for a cheap price
    • 3) Mapped out all of the places to stay, visit, and activities
    • 4) Found a route that let me see everything possible within the time span of my vacation

    After saying enough is enough, I set out to create an algorithm, this travel algorithm, to streamline the planning process. I don't promise that this website will cure all travel ills, but I hope it helps make your trip as awesome as possible in as little planning as possible!
