Add Start and End Dates / Locations

In order to start creating your plan, click on 'Change Dates / Locations'. If you haven't already, enter at least one Start or End date (if only a start or end date is entered, the number of travel days will be based off of the number of hours of activities and locations). If you have a set Start and/or End location, enter that as well, otherwise these locations will be determined based off of the most time efficient trip.


Add Activities and Locations to Stay

This is where you start adding the different elements of your trip. Want to add scuba diving? Perfect; add in the location of the dive, along with either the time it will take to scuba dive OR the fact that you're scuba diving. The scuba diving option will automatically fill in the length of time for the activity. Instead of adding an activity, you can also add a place you'd rather stay the night, and how many nights you'd prefer to stay there. The Length of stay isn't necessary, but if you'd prefer to stay in a certain location for a set amount of nights it's best to put the number of days. If no length of stay is included, it will be computed based on the activities. Once you've added an activity or a place to stay, simply add it to the plan to continue.


Your Planned Journey

Once some activities and locations have started being added, your journey will start being formulated. Within this table you'll have your whole list of activites and locations, along with a rating value of 1-5 on how badly you want this item to happen. Based off of your timeline, activities and locations will be chosen based off of rating, routes, and what's humanly possible to do in your time frame. Once you have all of the activities and locations that you can possibly hope to do, click the Create My Journey in order to run the algorithm and create your journey!


Congratulations! You have a Journey!

Now that you have the journey, feel free to use our website to start booking reservations, or one of our partner sites. And then get out there and have an adventure!
